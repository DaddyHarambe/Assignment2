In my eyes much of this group project was a success and working with my fellow group members was an ease. One thing I have learned about working within this group is how effective and efficiently work can be done when everyone contributes and dedicate themselves to their part of the work. As long as there was a voice to guide the group anything is possible and in general not that complicated.

Goup's Thoughts

As a group everyone worked proficiently and effectively. Everything was done with a purpose and time was mostly spent well. Although was noted previously it was difficult to gather all group members to conduct a meeting has each group member had a constraint on a given day. Although, we got through this and managed to conduct a few group meetings. Johnson was able to contact an IT specialist to conduct for the interview. John also worked efficiently to complete the website. It is also thanks to Lisa for her project idea and contribution to the IT technology component of the assignment that we were all able to complete this assignment. One thing that could have been improved on as a group is being more committed to the work. There were times where the work felt difficult to begin and unmotivating to do. As a group we should have motivated each other to push through the struggles and worked together more in general. The group also agrees on how there was much to learn through this experience. One of the primary lessons we learnt as a group is that it is difficult for one person to solely work on a component of the assignment by themselves. When two people are not bouncing ideas between each other it becomes stagnant and difficult to generate ideas. It was also critical that our group had a person to ensure who was doing what and relayed to the rest of the team what was required of them. It became distressful when each member did not know what to do during a given period. Another thing that went well during the group project was how willing each group member was to ask questions when confused or stuck on a situation. There was not a time where one of our members did not know what to do or understand a component of the assignment. Communication within the team was clear and everyone was comfortable with each other. All in all, working within a team has been a great experience for all of us. Everyone took responsibility for their actions at each critical moment of the assignment. There is still much to learn as our group is still new. Furthermore, another major lesson we can learn from and take from this experience is to always ensure everyone else is doing the right thing even if it’s a difficult decision and to check in on members from time to time and see how far they have gotten.
